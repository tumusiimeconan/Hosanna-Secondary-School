Academics at Hosana

At Hosana Secondary School, we are committed to providing a rigorous and comprehensive academic program that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond. Our curriculum, aligned with Uganda's national standards, offers a balanced blend of core subjects—including Mathematics, Sciences, English, and Social Studies—along with electives like ICT, Agriculture, and Fine Arts to foster creativity and practical skills.

Innovation

Our dedicated teachers employ innovative teaching methods, ensuring personalized attention to help every student excel. We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and character development, equipping students to become well-rounded individuals ready to tackle global challenges.

Hands On Learning

With state-of-the-art science labs, a well-stocked library, and regular academic assessments, Hosana Secondary School is the ideal place for your child to achieve academic excellence and lifelong learning.
